Professional Roof Restoration in Central Florida

MBR Roofing specializes in cost-effective roof restoration services throughout Central Florida. Our restoration solutions can extend the life of your existing roof by 10-15 years while saving you 60-80% compared to full replacement costs.

Benefits of Roof Restoration

  • Cost Savings: 60-80% less expensive than full replacement
  • Extended Lifespan: Add 10-15 years to your existing roof
  • Minimal Disruption: Faster completion than full replacement
  • Energy Efficiency: Improve insulation and reduce cooling costs
  • Environmentally Friendly: Reduce waste and landfill impact

Our Restoration Services

  • Tile Roof Restoration: Clean, repair, and re-coat tile roofs
  • Shingle Roof Restoration: Extend life of asphalt shingle roofs
  • Metal Roof Restoration: Repair and re-coat metal roofing
  • Commercial Restoration: Large-scale commercial projects
  • Preventive Maintenance: Regular inspections and maintenance
