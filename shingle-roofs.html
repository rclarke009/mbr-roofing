Quality Shingle Roofing in Central Florida

MBR Roofing specializes in installing and repairing durable, affordable shingle roofs for homes and businesses throughout Orlando, Seminole, and Central Florida. Our shingle roofing solutions provide excellent protection and curb appeal.

Why Choose Shingle Roofs?

  • Cost-Effective: Most affordable roofing option for most properties
  • Durable: 20-30 year lifespan with proper maintenance
  • Versatile: Available in many colors and styles
  • Easy to Repair: Individual shingles can be replaced as needed
  • Energy Efficient: Modern shingles offer excellent insulation

Our Shingle Roofing Services

  • New Installation: Complete shingle roof systems
  • Roof Replacement: Full tear-off and new installation
  • Repairs: Fix leaks, damaged shingles, and flashing
  • Maintenance: Regular inspections and preventive care
  • Emergency Services: Storm damage and urgent repairs
