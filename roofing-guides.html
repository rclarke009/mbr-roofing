Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend inspecting your roof at least twice a year - once in spring and once in fall. Additionally, inspect after any major storms or severe weather events.

Common signs include: shingles that are curling, buckling, or missing; granules in gutters; daylight visible through roof boards; sagging roof deck; and water stains on ceilings.

Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on the size and complexity. Commercial projects may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
