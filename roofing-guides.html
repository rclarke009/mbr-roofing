Essential Roofing Guides

Knowledge is power when it comes to protecting your biggest investment. Our roofing guides help Central Florida homeowners and business owners understand roof maintenance, materials, and when to call in the professionals.

Basic Maintenance

  • Regular gutter cleaning and inspection
  • Checking for missing or damaged shingles
  • Monitoring for water stains on ceilings
  • Annual professional inspection recommended

When to Call a Pro

  • Visible water damage or leaks
  • Missing or curling shingles
  • Granule loss in gutters
  • Roof age over 15-20 years

Storm Damage Signs

  • Hail damage to shingles
  • Wind uplift or missing materials
  • Debris impact damage
  • Immediate inspection after major storms

Central Florida Specific

  • UV protection for intense sun exposure
  • Hurricane-rated materials
  • Proper ventilation for humidity
  • Regular cleaning for algae and moss
